Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelago in Southeast Asia. Known for its rich culture, diverse wildlife, and beautiful beaches, it is a popular destination for most tourists. The country is consist of 3 major parts which is Luzon at the top that consists of mountainous and flat land formations that were used for agriculture, Visayas in the middle part which consists of small island groups and lastly Mindanao at the bottom that consists of large land mass but rich in sealife.
